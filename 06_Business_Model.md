# Monetization & Business Model

## Freemium Model
- Basic (Free): Pomodoro, To-Do list, gamification, max 50 pages PDF upload/month.
- Neura Pro (Paid): Unlimited AI processing, Socratic tutor, detailed analytics. Priced affordably (equivalent to the cost of one private tutoring session per month).
- Family Plan: Discounted bundle for households with multiple students.

## B2B Integrations
- Event Sponsors: Universities and event organizers (TEDx, NASA Space Apps) can sponsor localized, targeted events shown to students based on their study interests.
- Neura Creators: Tutors can create verified accounts to upload their materials (Flashcards/Quizzes) and invite their physical students to practice on the app using affiliate codes.
- AI Caching: To reduce API costs, if a student uploads a known generic PDF (e.g., a standard ministry book), the server serves cached AI results instead of reprocessing.
