# Security & Privacy Requirements

## Data Protection
- Data Anonymization: Study habits, sleep data, and scores must be anonymized (hashed tokens) on the server.
- COPPA & Local Compliance: Clear, simple terms in Egyptian Arabic ensuring data is not sold to ad agencies.

## Parental Control (Trust but Verify)
- Parents receive a high-level weekly "Saver Report" (via WhatsApp API or Parent app view) showing focused hours and money saved on tutoring.
- Parents CANNOT see private chats with Neura or individual mistakes to maintain the app as a "Safe Space" for the student.

## Socratic Guardrails (Anti-Cheating)
- The AI prompt must be strictly instructed: "DO NOT solve homework for the user. Act as a Socratic tutor. Provide hints, explain concepts, and guide the user to find the answer themselves."
