# Project Name: Neura (نيورا)
## Core Concept
Neura is the first AI-powered study companion designed specifically for Egyptian students (High School & University). It relies on Neuroscience and Cognitive Psychology (inspired by "Learning How to Learn"). It combines time management, AI study tools, health tracking, and gamification to replace expensive private tutoring.

## Target Audience
- Egyptian High School Students (Thanaweya Amma).
- Egyptian University Students.

## AI Persona (Neura)
- Tone of Voice: Friendly, motivating "Big Brother/Coach".
- Language: Educated Egyptian Arabic (العامية المصرية الراقية) mixed with simple scientific terms.
- Interaction: Text chat and AI-generated Voice Notes.
- Dynamic Avatar: A 2D/3D character whose mood changes based on the student's consistency and streak.

## Core Value Proposition (North Star Metric)
Increase "Voluntary Deep Work Hours" by transforming studying from a stressful burden into an engaging, gamified daily habit.
