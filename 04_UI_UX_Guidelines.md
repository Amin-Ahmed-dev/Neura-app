# UI/UX Design System

## Visual Identity
- Style: Minimalist, Gamer-stat inspired, zero-distraction.
- Dark Mode: REQUIRED as the default mode (most target users study at night).
- Colors:
  - Deep Blue/Navy (#0F172A): Primary background (promotes focus).
  - Emerald Green (#10B981): Success, Streaks, completed tasks.
  - Coral/Orange (#F97316): Alerts, Active Pomodoro timer.

## Layout Rules
- No infinite scrolling to prevent dopamine distraction.
- Dashboard: Must show "Deep Work Time", "Fluency Meter", and "Current Streak".
- Frictionless Entry: "Start Next Task" giant button on the home screen to eliminate decision fatigue.
- Grayscale Nudge: UI turns black & white 30 mins before bedtime to reduce screen time.
